Getting Started

Before you begin…

Follow these three guidelines for the best animations from your doodles.

Guideline 1 / 3

Good lighting & contrast

Make sure your doodle stands out clearly from the background. Dark, bold lines on white or light paper in good lighting give the best results.

Use natural light or a bright room

Guideline 2 / 3

2 arms & 2 legs required

The animation engine detects and moves each limb individually. Your character needs two clearly visible arms and two legs — any creature shape works!

Humans, animals, monsters all work!

Guideline 3 / 3

One character at a time

Our AI focuses all its magic on a single character. Multiple figures, busy backgrounds, or crowded scenes will confuse the detection — keep it to just one.

Pro tip: Keep it simple

Bold outlines on white paper always give the best animations. Simple shapes outperform detailed, heavily colored drawings every time.

The following data may be collected when you use the App:

  • Name and email address (if you create an account)
  • Anonymous app usage statistics
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Crash reports and performance data

3. How We Use Your Data

Collected data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Ensuring the App works correctly
  • Improving the user experience
  • Identifying and resolving technical issues
  • Notifying you about important updates

4. Data Sharing

Your personal data is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties unless required by law. Only anonymous usage statistics may be analysed to improve service quality.

5. Data Security

Your data is protected using industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL). Technical and administrative measures are in place to prevent unauthorised access.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to know whether your data is being processed
  • Right to request information about how it is processed
  • Right to have incorrect data corrected
  • Right to request deletion of your data

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a child, we will delete it immediately.
